# Initiation Checklist

**Last Updated:** 2026-02-08

Use this checklist before sharing an Initiation Brief.

1. **Problem statement is problem-first (not solution-first).**
2. **Business case + "why now" are stated in 1–3 bullets.**
3. **Goals include at least one measurable success metric.** *(See STAGES.md: measurement throughline)*
4. **Scope boundaries are explicit (in-scope and out-of-scope).**
5. **Non-goals are explicit.** *(See `initiation-reference.md`: Non-goals)*
6. **Top assumptions (≤5) are written down.** *(See `initiation-reference.md`: Assumptions)*
7. **Top risks/unknowns (≤5) are written down with likelihood + mitigations.** *(See `initiation-reference.md`: Risks)*
8. **Constraints and dependencies are identified with owners where possible.**
9. **At least one alternative option was considered.** *(See `initiation-reference.md`: Options)*
10. **Hypothesis includes a time range and confidence level.** *(See `initiation-reference.md`: Estimation)*
11. **Gate 1 (Initiation approval) decision criteria are explicit.** *(See `initiation-reference.md`: Gate)*
12. **An owner/DRI is named.** *(See `initiation-reference.md`: DRI)*

> If items 1–4, 10–12 are missing, the brief is usually not ready to circulate.

---

## Exit Criteria Alignment

This checklist aligns with the formal exit criteria in [STAGES.md](../../STAGES.md#stage-1-initiation):
- Stakeholders approve business case and objectives (items 1–2)
- Success metrics are defined and measurable (item 3)
- Scope boundaries are clear (items 4–5)
- Go/no-go decision made (item 11)
