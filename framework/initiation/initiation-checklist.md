# Initiation Checklist

**Last Updated:** 2026-02-08

Use this checklist before sharing an Initiation Brief.

1. **Problem statement is problem-first (not solution-first).**
2. **Business case + "why now" are stated in 1–3 bullets.**
3. **Goals include at least one measurable success metric.** _(See STAGES.md:
   measurement throughline)_
4. **Scope boundaries are explicit (in-scope and out-of-scope).**
5. **Non-goals are explicit.** _(See `initiation-guide.md`: Non-goals)_
6. **Top assumptions (≤5) are written down.** _(See `initiation-guide.md`:
   Assumptions)_
7. **Top risks/unknowns (≤5) are written down with likelihood + mitigations.**
   _(See `initiation-guide.md`: Risks)_
8. **Constraints and dependencies are identified with owners where possible.**
9. **At least one alternative option was considered.** _(See
   `initiation-guide.md`: Options)_
10. **Hypothesis includes a time range and confidence level.** _(See
    `initiation-guide.md`: Estimation)_
11. **Gate 1 (Initiation approval) decision criteria are explicit.** _(See
    `initiation-guide.md`: Gate)_
12. **An owner/DRI is named.** _(See `initiation-guide.md`: DRI)_

> If items 1–4, 10–12 are missing, the brief is usually not ready to circulate.

---

## Exit Criteria Alignment

This checklist aligns with the formal exit criteria in
[STAGES.md](../../STAGES.md#stage-1-initiation):

- Stakeholders approve business case and objectives (items 1–2)
- Success metrics are defined and measurable (item 3)
- Scope boundaries are clear (items 4–5)
- Go/no-go decision made (item 11)
